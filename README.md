# Energy-Consumption-and-GDP-Analysis

## Dataset
The dataset presents global energy consumption and sustainable energy usage data from 2000 to 2020. It includes annual statistics for each country, such as GDP, population, energy types,nand renewable energy usage. The data enables analysis of energy trends, economic relations, and sustainability progress across different regions and countries worldwide.

Ansh Tanwar. (2023). Global Data on Sustainable Energy (2000-2020) [Data set]. Kaggle. https://doi.org/10.34740/KAGGLE/DSV/6327347
